# Phase 2: Data-Preservation Campaign

Research date: July 16, 2026

Status: Draft package only. No messages have been sent.

## Purpose and guardrails

This phase is designed to prevent potentially recoverable Save This Life records from being deleted while ownership and authority are investigated. The initial communications request preservation and routing only. They do not request disclosure, assert that the sender owns the records, threaten litigation, or claim that a recipient is legally obligated to comply.

Before sending any document labeled a legal hold, subpoena, demand, or notice of anticipated litigation, obtain review from licensed counsel. A voluntary preservation request may be ignored and generally does not substitute for valid legal process.

Do not include pet-owner personal information, full microchip lists, credentials, access tokens, or speculative accusations in initial communications. Use business and professional channels only.

## Exit criteria for Phase 2

Phase 2 is complete when every evidence-supported custodian has either confirmed preservation/routing, stated that it is not a custodian, or failed to respond after documented follow-up, and counsel has decided whether enforceable process is justified. A message being sent is not itself proof that records were preserved.
